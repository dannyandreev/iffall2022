Title: Shakshuka
description
The shakshuka is an easy, healthy breakfast (or any time of day) recipe popular in Israel and other parts of the Middle East and North Africa. It’s a simple combination of simmering tomatoes, onions, garlic, spices and gently poached eggs. It’s nourishing, filling and one recipe I guarantee you’ll make time and again.
ingredients
  • tomatoe
  • egg
  • onion
  • red bell pepper
  • garlic
  • spices
  • salt
  • parsley
  • cilantro
  • Olive Oil
instructions
  1. Chop tomatoes
  2. Saute onions with olive oil
  3. dice red bell pepper
  4. mix tomatoes, onions, bell pepper and let simmer
  5. Add spices and garlic
  6. crack eggs on top
  7. once eggs are the right consistency turn off the heat
  8. sprinkle on some salt, parsley and cilatro and serve hot
